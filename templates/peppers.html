Cream Cheese Stuffed Peppers

Prep Time: 1 hour
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Total Time: 1 hour and 10 minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 8 bell peppers
  • 10 cups spinach
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 4oz of mushrooms
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 4 8oz blocks of cream cheese
  • 2 cups mozzeralla cheese
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 tsp. paprika

Instructions

  1. Dice the onion, mushrooms, and garlic and saute in a pan over med-high heat.
  2. Add spinach to the pan and cover the pan. Wait until they and sweated down and add more. Repeat until all the spinach has been added.
  3. Pre-heat the oven to 350 degrees.
  4. Add the onions, mushrooms, garlic, and spinach to a large mixing bowl. Set aside some of the mozzeralla cheese to cover the peppers add the rest to the bowl. Add the cream cheese and parmesan cheese to the bowl and mix all together.
  5. Add paprika and mix in salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Cut the peppers in half and remove the stem and seeds.
  7. Set the peppers on a baking sheet and fill each pepper with the mix from the bowl.
  8. Cover the peppers with the remaining mozzeralla cheese.
  9. Once done pre-heated, add the peppers to the oven. Wait until the cheese is golden-brown or about 10 minutes.
  10. Enjoy!
